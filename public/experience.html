Projects


  • Predictive NBA Shot Modeling (See Here)
    • Working with Andrew M., we combined NBA shot logs with player physicals and defender information to train XGboost and SVM models to predict the make/miss probability of a shot taken by a given NBA player. We found the accuracy of these models to be higher than that of previous studies done at the time of our project: March, 2024. These models were then utilized to create a useful function which generates a scouting report that can tell a team who they should have guard a given player based on how the models predict that player would shoot (%) against a given defender.

  • "Hub Bucks Simulator" (See Here)
    • At UPS you can earn an in-warehouse currency called hub bucks for a variety of actions, such as catching bad packages - something I did as a loader at UPS in the summer of 2022. Following that experience, I built out a simulation and accompanying shiny app in RStudio utilizing monte-carlo statistical methods to estimate how many hub bucks one might earn while working at UPS depending on several factors such as: package flow rate, bad package chance, etc.
